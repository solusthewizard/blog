// about

A blog about building things well.

byte//sized is a technical blog focused on the craft of it infrastructure. It's a place for long-form writing about the decisions, tradeoffs, and hard-won lessons.

Topics range from choosing hardware for replacing VMware cluster with hyperconverged solutions to what we are doing to replace software delivery solutions like App-V. The goal is writing that's hopefully genuinely useful — not watered down, not padded out.

Who writes here

This blog is written by a small group of engineers who care about their work. We have a passion for sharing what we've learned and helping others navigate the complex world of IT infrastructure.
