// about

A blog about building things well.

byte//sized is a technical blog focused on the craft of software engineering. It's a place for long-form writing about the decisions, tradeoffs, and hard-won lessons that don't fit in a tweet or a Stack Overflow answer.

Topics range from language deep-dives and architecture patterns to developer tooling, testing philosophy, and the occasional opinion piece about how we work. The goal is writing that's genuinely useful — not watered down, not padded out.
